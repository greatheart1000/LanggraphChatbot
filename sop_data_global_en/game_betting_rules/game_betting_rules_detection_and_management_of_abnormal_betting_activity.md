# Game & Betting Rules - Detection and Management of Abnormal Betting Activity

## Steps

1. **Monitor betting activity for abnormal patterns**
   - Continuously review betting behaviors, especially on live games, for signs of irregular or suspicious activity.
   - Detect repeated, unusually high, or atypical betting patterns, as well as suspicious IP activity.
   - Be aware that the system automatically flags abnormal betting activity based on predefined criteria (which vary per site according to configuration).

2. **Identify flagged accounts**
   - When a betting activity triggers the system's detection, verify if the account has been flagged for abnormal or irregular betting.
   - Check for indications such as unusual betting patterns, suspicious IP addresses, or abnormal profits from irregular activity.

3. **Inform the player of detection and explain potential consequences**
   - Notify the player that their betting activity has been flagged for abnormal patterns, in accordance with the site's internal policies.
   - Clarify that:
     - Profits gained from abnormal betting may be subject to deduction.
     - Continued abnormal activity could lead to further restrictions or account suspension.
     - The review process may involve an internal audit of betting history.

4. **Assess the player's betting activity and enforce restrictions if necessary**
   - Require the player to continue wagering in allowed game types (e.g., slot games) to complete any required turnover, if applicable.
   - Advise the player that withdrawals may be delayed or paused until the review is completed and the activity is verified as compliant with the rules.
   - Remind the player to bet normally and avoid further irregular activity to prevent penalties.

5. **Review betting history and system flags**
   - Conduct an internal review of the accountâ€™s betting history, focusing on:
     - Patterns that align with flagged activity.
     - Any evidence of malicious or illegal software use.
   - Confirm if the betting activity aligns with the criteria for abnormal or irregular activity as per the current site's configuration.

6. **Apply appropriate sanctions or restrictions**
   - If abnormal betting activity is confirmed:
     - Deduct any profits associated with such activity, as per the system's anti-fraud policies.
     - Temporarily freeze the account if required, pending further review.
     - Communicate the outcome clearly to the player, including any deductions or restrictions applied.
     - For malicious or illegal activity, consider account suspension or other corrective actions per policy.

7. **Process withdrawals after activity normalization**
   - Allow withdrawal only after:
     - The player has completed the required betting activity or turnover on permitted game types.
     - The internal review confirms that betting activity is in compliance.
   - Ensure that any flagged profits are appropriately deducted prior to releasing funds.

8. **Handle system-initiated delays or closures of game types**
   - Recognize that certain game types, especially live games or lottery games, may be temporarily delayed, closed for maintenance, or subject to draw delays.
   - Communicate clearly to players if delays occur due to official draws or system updates, and advise on subsequent steps.

9. **Maintain records of detected activity and actions taken**
   - Log all detection instances, reviews, and sanctions applied.
   - Document the rationale for restrictions, deductions, or account suspensions for auditing and transparency.

## Notes

- The specific thresholds and criteria for detection, restrictions, or deductions depend on each site's current policies and configuration; always refer to the latest official rules.
- The detection system may automatically deduct profits from irregular betting activities, including malicious arbitrage or illegal betting.
- Continued or extreme irregular betting activity may result in further penalties, including permanent account suspension, in accordance with internal policies.

## Common questions

- How do we determine if betting activity is abnormal?
  - Based on predefined criteria configured per site, including betting patterns, IP activity, or profit anomalies.

- What should be communicated to the player?
  - Players should be informed that abnormal betting detected may lead to restrictions, profit deductions, or account review, and that they may need to complete certain betting requirements to proceed with withdrawals.

- When can the player withdraw after abnormal activity is detected?
  - Only after completing required betting activity on permitted games and verification that activities are compliant with the platform's policies.

- Are there different procedures for different game types?
  - Yes, certain restrictions or delays may be specific to game types such as live games, lottery, or casino games; follow the configuration and official policies accordingly.
