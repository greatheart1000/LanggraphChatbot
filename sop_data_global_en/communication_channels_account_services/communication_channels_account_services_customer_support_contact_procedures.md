# Communication Channels & Account Services - Customer Support Contact Procedures

## Steps

1. **Identify the player’s issue and gather initial details:**
   - Ask the player to clearly describe the problem they are experiencing.
   - Request relevant transaction details such as amounts, payment methods used, or reference codes if applicable.
   - Instruct the player to provide supporting evidence such as screenshots or videos demonstrating the issue.
   - For deposit or withdrawal issues, request the relevant transaction records or receipts, ensuring references, dates, and times are clear.

2. **Verify the player's account information and contact details:**
   - Confirm the player's account username or ID.
   - Ask the player to verify current contact information bound to their account, such as email or phone number.
   - Guide the player on how to bind or update contact information if necessary, which typically requires direct communication with support and verification procedures (e.g., submission of identity documents and selfies).

3. **Determine the preferred and supported communication channels:**
   - Instruct the player to contact support through official channels supported by the platform, which may include:
     - Live chat via the website or app.
     - Official social media channels (if specified by the site).
     - Email, if applicable.
     - Specific in-platform support features such as a support button or agent link.
   - Advise that support will not initiate outbound calls to solicit personal or sensitive information.

4. **Guide the player on contacting support efficiently:**
   - If using live chat, direct the player to access the live chat feature on the platform.
   - For social media or other channels, confirm the official handles or groups as per the current platform’s support policy.
   - Remind the player to provide their account username, a detailed problem description, and relevant transaction or incident references during contact.

5. **Request supporting documentation or evidence if applicable:**
   - Ask the player to attach screenshots, transaction records, or receipts related to the issue.
   - Ensure they provide matching reference numbers, dates, and amounts for deposits or withdrawals if relevant.
   - Advise that providing comprehensive evidence helps expedite resolution.

6. **Communicate the expected operating hours and support limitations:**
   - Inform the player that support availability depends on current operational hours, which should be communicated clearly.
   - Note that outside these hours, support may be limited or unavailable, and the player may need to wait until support resumes.

7. **Follow up and verify resolution steps:**
   - Confirm that the player has provided all requested information.
   - Assist with further clarification if needed.
   - Coordinate internal checks or operations, such as verifying transaction records or account status, using supported back-end tools according to the platform’s policies.

8. **Advise the player on resolution or explanation:**
   - Based on gathered information and internal checks, provide an appropriate explanation or resolution in accordance with the platform's current rules and configurations.
   - If a process for updating contact details or resolving issues is needed, guide the player through the necessary verification steps, including submission of identity documents and supporting evidence, following the platform’s official procedures.

## Notes

- Always use official support channels such as LiveChat, verified social media accounts, or official email addresses.
- Support agents should never request or handle personal or sensitive information via unsolicited calls or messages.
- The exact procedures and thresholds for account verification or resolution may vary between sites; refer to current site-specific policies and official support documentation.
- When instructing players on updating contact details or submitting verification, ensure clarity about the documentation required and the verification process.
